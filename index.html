Notes:

This calculator is meant only as a simple guide. This will NOT depict true numbers; however, the calculations are set for worst-case scenarios.

For worst case calculations, it assumes (weekly) you can have withdrawals up to five times a month but only have four deposits. The same assumption applies to bi-weekly transfers - up to three withdrawals and minimum of two deposits. As for monthly,...it's just "one" month;

Using the above principal, the final weekly savings calculation takes in to account the possibility of a five-week month.
